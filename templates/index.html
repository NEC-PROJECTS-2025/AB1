About the Project

This project focuses on predicting future outcomes using advanced machine learning techniques.

Our research, titled "Boosting Network Intrusion Detection with Two-Level Ensemble Learning and Knowledge Distillation Approaches," is a collaborative effort spearheaded by myself, Macharla Bala Rangarao, alongside my esteemed co-authors. This work is conducted under the expert guidance of Dr. S.V.N. Sreenivasu at Narasaraopeta Engineering College. This study aims to revolutionize traditional Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS) by addressing their limitations through an innovative, AI-driven framework. By leveraging two-level ensemble learning and knowledge distillation techniques, we seek to enhance detection accuracy, reduce computational overhead, and ensure robust network security. Our research reflects our commitment to advancing cybersecurity and contributing meaningful solutions to the ever-evolving challenges in the field.
